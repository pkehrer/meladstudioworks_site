Melad StudioWorks designs and creates handmade modern furniture and casework in rural central Massachusetts. We offer complete custom design and fabrication for new pieces, carry a limited in-stock inventory, or can build to your specs. We strive to produce the best work possible using high quality materials and traditional techniques, while at the same time maintaining a modern aesthetic.

You can purchase most in stock items at our Etsy store, or email directly for custom options.
